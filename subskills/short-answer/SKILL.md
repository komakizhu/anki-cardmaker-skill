# Short-Answer Sub-skill / 简答题子模块

Use for conceptual questions that require a concise answer, especially Chinese professional-study material.

- Use `type: QA` and `question_type: short_answer`.
- Keep the front focused on one question and the back concise but sufficient.
- For Chinese non-language-learning topics, keep the question, answer, explanation, and mnemonic in Chinese whenever possible.
- Do not add English vocabulary modules unless the user requests English-learning treatment.
- Apply the shared two-stage preview and explicit-import gate.
